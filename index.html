Services

  • Engine Repair and Maintenance

    Our experienced technicians can diagnose and repair any issues with your car or big machine's engine, including replacing worn-out parts and performing routine maintenance to keep it running smoothly.

  • Transmission Repair and Replacement

    If your car or big machine's transmission is giving you trouble, bring it to us for expert diagnosis and repair. We can also replace your transmission if necessary, to keep your vehicle running smoothly.

    Properly functioning brakes are crucial for your safety on the road or job site. Our brake services include inspection, repair, and replacement of brake pads, rotors, and other components to ensure your vehicle stops reliably.

  • Suspension and Steering Repair

    Our suspension and steering services include alignment, shock and strut replacement, and repair of steering components to ensure your vehicle handles properly and rides smoothly.

  • Electrical and Computer Diagnostics

    Modern vehicles and machines are equipped with advanced computer systems that control everything from the engine to the entertainment system. Our diagnostic services use the latest tools and techniques to diagnose and repair any issues with your vehicle's electrical and computer systems.

    Don't let a broken AC or heating system make your driving or working experience uncomfortable. Our technicians can diagnose and repair issues with your vehicle's heating and cooling system, including recharge and replacement of refrigerant and other components.
